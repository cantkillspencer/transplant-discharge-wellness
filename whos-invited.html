Who's Invited

Who Is This Training For?

Built for clinicians who refuse to let care end at the bedside.

This training is built for transplant staff who are ready to be part of something new — the Transplant Discharge Wellness Group. This is a community of clinicians who believe that exceptional patient care doesn't end at the bedside. It extends through the front door, into the home, and into the first critical weeks after discharge.

If you are committed to raising the standard of post-discharge care for transplant patients and are ready to demonstrate that commitment through action, this group is for you.

A Note on Who Is Not Invited: This training is not designed for those who do not believe they are capable of — or ready to commit to — the elevated level of care that transplant patients require. This is not a judgment. It is a boundary. The patients we serve cannot afford anything less.

Our Invitation to You

Your first act of membership begins before you arrive.

Before you arrive at training, we ask you to complete a short pre-work module. It is not designed to be a 'test' — but to prime your perspective. The scenario presented actually happened, and it happened right here, in our hospital.

It will never happen again because of the work you will be doing as a founding member of the Transplant Discharge Wellness Team. You will be asked to put someone you love into that story. And then you will be asked to explain, in your own words, how it happened and what were the precursors that allowed it to happen.

This is your first act of membership in the Transplant Discharge Wellness Group.
